About Us

Robotic Knee Replacement at
CTS Hospital

Robotic knee replacement is a breakthrough surgical technique that combines advanced robotics with expert orthopedic care to deliver highly precise, computer-assisted knee implants. At CTS Speciality Hospital, this innovative technology allows for personalized, minimally invasive surgery that improves joint function, reduces post-op pain, and speeds up recovery.

As Anna Nagar’s first center to offer robotic-assisted knee surgery, CTS is proud to lead the way in next-gen orthopedic care — helping patients walk confidently into a better quality of life.
